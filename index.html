Categories

Explore various categories of coffee making resources.

Espresso-Based Drinks

These include coffee drinks that are made with espresso as the base. Espresso is a concentrated form of coffee produced by forcing hot water under high pressure through finely-ground coffee beans

Espresso

A concentrated form of coffee served in small, strong shots.

Cappuccino

Made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam.

Latte

Composed of espresso and steamed milk, topped with a small amount of milk foam.

Brewed Coffee

These are traditional coffee preparations where coffee grounds are brewed with hot water.

Drip Coffee

Made by dripping boiling water over ground coffee, which then passes through a filter.

French Press

Coffee grounds are steeped in hot water and then separated by pressing a plunger through a mesh filter.

Pour Over

Hot water is poured over coffee grounds in a filter, allowing the coffee to drip into a carafe or mug below.

Sample Resources

Here are some steps you can try at home

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of water
  • 1-2 tablespoons of ground coffee (depending on desired strength)

Steps:

  • Boil Water: Bring the water to a boil in a kettle or pot.
  • Add Coffee Grounds: Place the coffee grounds in a coffee maker or a French press.
  • Pour Water: Pour the boiling water over the coffee grounds.
  • Brew: Allow the coffee to brew for 4-5 minutes.
  • Serve: Pour the brewed coffee into a cup and enjoy.

Ingredients:

  • 1 shot of espresso (about 1 ounce)
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup of milk foam

Steps:

  • Brew Espresso: Use an espresso machine to brew a shot of espresso.
  • Steam Milk: Steam the milk using the steam wand of your espresso machine until it's hot and frothy.
  • Pour Milk: Pour the steamed milk into the cup with the espresso.
  • Add Foam: Spoon the milk foam on top of the steamed milk.
  • Serve: Optionally, sprinkle some cocoa powder or cinnamon on top and enjoy.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of strong brewed coffee (cooled)
  • Ice cubes
  • Milk or cream (optional)
  • Sweetener (optional)

Steps:

  • Brew Coffee: Brew a strong cup of coffee and let it cool to room temperature.
  • Fill Glass with Ice: Fill a tall glass with ice cubes.
  • Pour Coffee: Pour the cooled coffee over the ice.
  • Add Milk/Cream: Add milk or cream to taste if desired.
  • Sweeten: Add sweetener to taste if desired.
  • Serve: Stir well and enjoy your refreshing iced coffee.
